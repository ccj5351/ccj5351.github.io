2. Result Analysis

Possible Reasons:

  • The residual images have been reduced, after an appropriate normalization is adopted, i.e. intensity 0 maps to 127, and the maximal absolute intensity (i.e., positive and nagative) maps to 255 and 0. For example, with compression quality being 5, the residual of category Beijing National Stadium, was reduced from 1.59MB to 391KB, but still not accepted.
  • Some threshold and other compressor could also be applied to the residual between the original image and the reconstructed one, before it is further compressed, to reduce the size of the residual.
