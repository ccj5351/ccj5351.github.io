1. Description:

  • This experiment aimed at finding an appropriate epitome size, in terms of PSNR and RMSE.
  • This experiment learned 4 images of a building, named as sub-Facade-less dataset, with the same size of 1024 by 768 for each image.
  • The PSNR and RMSE results are achieved among original images and the epitomic reconstruction. Here the residual have not been considered, due to our purpose of only finding the appropriate epitome size.

2. Result Analysis

  • "200-recon4" can get the better reconstruction quality, that is we set the epitome width (= height) as 200 pixels, and recon4 means the patch spacing is 4 pixels (overlapping).
  • "200-recon8" will cause boundary blurring (recon8 = non overlapping).
  • "200" is better than "57", "100", and "160". And "200" is good enough. Larger epitome might gain better reconstruction quality, but the overhead will also increase.
